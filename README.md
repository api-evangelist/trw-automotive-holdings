# TRW Automotive Holdings

TRW Automotive Holdings was one of the world's largest automotive parts suppliers, specializing
in active and passive safety systems including braking systems, steering systems, suspension
systems, seat belts, airbags, and advanced driver assistance systems (ADAS). TRW Automotive
was acquired by ZF Friedrichshafen AG in 2015 and integrated into ZF Group as ZF TRW.
